# Three Non-Microsoft Alternatives to Microsoft Publisher

1. **Affinity Publisher**  
   Excellent for professional brochures, flyers, newsletters, and magazines. Strong replacement for Publisher with advanced layout tools and no Microsoft dependency.

2. **Canva**  
   Very easy to use for flyers, church notices, social media graphics, cards, and posters. Great for quick design work with templates.

3. **Scribus**  
   Free and open-source option for creating professional page layouts, PDFs, newsletters, and print-ready documents. Good for budget-conscious users.
